The Methodology

In order to accurately focus on the user, we need to get working journeys in front of users as soon as possible. This allows us to test and optimise based on empirical evidence, opposed to a more 'pin the tail' methodology.

But its not only great in delivering a engaging UX, its also leaner and more agile, allowing us to quickly iterate, modify, improve and optimise.

UX is often seen as a catalyst for organisational change, this is because in order to deliver a truly great user experience, you need to continually measure and test improvements throughout design and development. To do this efficiently we need to have another look at how we approach some projects.
