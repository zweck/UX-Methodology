Research, Sketch and Plan

"If I'd asked people what they wanted, they would have said a faster horse."- Henry Ford

By conducting design research up front, we can try and eliminate some of the pain points later on. If you know who your users are, what the business goals are, and what the technological constrains are, you can design a solution that balances these constraints, ultimately giving rationale to decisions.

Using key principles from HCI (Human Computer Interaction); by researching the and organisational goals, we will form a basis from which to begin planning interactions. From here we will sketch elements and ideas using this research as a foundation. Sketches form a fundamental foundation, its low fidelity nature allows for quick iteration and conceptualisation.
